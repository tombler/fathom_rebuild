Change Agents Summer Cohort '16
Nashville High School Students  
July 25 - August 5     |     9:00 AM - 2:00 PM
TSU Avon Campus 
 
Change Agents is a Fathom project empowering young people with tools for social change.
 
This summer, Fathom is excited to offer 40 high school students across Nashville the opportunity to participate in our Change Agents Summer Cohort. Over the course of two weeks, students will explore topics like social justice, entrepreneurship and community sustainability, while developing their own personalized blueprints to pursue their passions and make positive impacts in their communities. Students will pitch their ideas for social change to a panel of experts and community stakeholders for the chance to win cash prizes and mentorship to help launch their ventures.
Know a Change Agent in your school or community? Nominate a student from the Greater Nashville Area for the Summer Cohort '16.
